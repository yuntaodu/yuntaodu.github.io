About Me

Currently I am working at Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI). I got Ph.D degree from Nanjing University in June 2023. I was a member of IIP Group, led by professor Chongjun Wang. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree from Northeastern University in June 2018. My research interests include topics in machine learning. Most recently, I am interested in transfer learning, domain adaptation, semi-supervised learning, and vision language learning.

We have collected many resources about transfer learning and have released them in the github.

Hint: Our group is looking for research intern, if you are intersted in Transfer Learning(TL), Large Language Model(LLM) or Vision-Language Model(VLM), you could contact me.
